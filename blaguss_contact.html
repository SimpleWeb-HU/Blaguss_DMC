Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Send us a message and we'll get back to you.

What is a Destination Management Company (DMC) and do I need one? −
A DMC is a local expert that handles all logistics, planning and execution for your event in a given destination. If you're organising a corporate event or incentive trip in Hungary and don't have a local team, a DMC ensures seamless delivery and access to venues and suppliers you couldn't reach on your own.
What types of events do you organise? +
We organise a wide range of events including incentive travel programmes, corporate team buildings, gala dinners, product launches, international congresses and conferences, as well as multi-day programmes combining cultural and business activities.
Do you work outside of Budapest? +
Yes — we operate across all of Hungary. While Budapest is our home base, we have deep knowledge of destinations like Lake Balaton, Eger, Tokaj, Pécs and Debrecen. We can also arrange cross-border programmes into neighbouring countries upon request.
How far in advance should we contact you? +
For large congresses or multi-day incentive programmes, we recommend getting in touch 6–12 months in advance. For smaller corporate events, 2–4 months is usually sufficient. That said, we're experienced in handling last-minute requests — don't hesitate to reach out regardless of timeline.
What is the difference between your DMC and PCO services? +
Our DMC services focus on destination logistics — venue sourcing, accommodation, transport, creative programmes and gala events — primarily for corporate and incentive groups. Our PCO services are designed for scientific and professional associations organising congresses, covering abstract management, registration systems, scientific programme coordination and financial management.
Can you handle both the congress programme and social events? +
Absolutely. This is where our combined DMC and PCO expertise becomes a real advantage. We can manage the full scientific programme alongside all social activities — welcome receptions, gala dinners, excursions and cultural programmes — under one roof.
Do you provide sustainability options for events? +
Yes. We are committed to responsible event planning. This includes sourcing from local suppliers, selecting venues with sustainability certifications, minimising single-use plastics, and offering carbon offset options. We can prepare a sustainability report for your event on request.
