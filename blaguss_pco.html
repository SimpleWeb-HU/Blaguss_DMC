Professional Congress Organisation

Hungary's Premier
Congress Organiser

Blaguss PCO supports scientific societies, associations and corporations in organising high-quality international congresses — aligned with their professional objectives and delivered with precision. From bid preparation to closing ceremony, our team manages every aspect of your congress: scientific programme, financial planning, registration, sponsorship and full on-site management.

Every congress is unique — and we treat it that way. Whether you are planning an intimate scientific symposium for 80 delegates or a major international congress for 2,000 participants, our approach is always tailored to your specific goals, budget and audience. Our experienced team works closely with scientific committees, keynote speakers and international delegates to ensure every congress reflects the highest academic and professional standards.

As proud members of IAPCO and ICCA, we operate in full compliance with international standards for congress organisation and financial management. With decades of experience and a strong European network, we have successfully delivered conferences and congresses across major European cities — from Budapest to Edinburgh, Copenhagen to Athens.

Founded in Budapest in 1984, Blaguss PCO has grown into one of Hungary's most trusted professional congress organisers. Our longstanding partnerships with international associations are built on transparency, reliability and an unwavering commitment to quality — from the first site inspection to the final financial report.
