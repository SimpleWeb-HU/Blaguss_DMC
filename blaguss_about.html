Who We Are

A Trusted Partner
in Every Detail

Blaguss Destination Management Kft. is Hungary's most established destination management and professional congress organising company. We combine deep local knowledge with international standards of service — a combination that has made us the preferred partner for some of the world's leading organisations.

Our team brings together specialists in event logistics, congress management, venue sourcing, creative programming, and client relations. Every project — regardless of scale — receives the same level of attention and commitment. We don't just deliver events. We craft experiences that leave lasting impressions on delegates, clients and sponsors alike.

Our work spans the full event spectrum: from intimate executive retreats for 20 guests to international medical congresses bringing together 3,000 delegates from 60 countries. We have organised gala dinners in Budapest's grandest palaces, incentive programmes across Hungary's wine regions, and scientific congresses in world-class venues. In each case, our role is to make the complex simple — and the ordinary extraordinary.

As part of the international Blaguss Group, we have access to an unmatched network of resources, suppliers and regional expertise. This gives our clients a single, trusted point of contact for everything — from airport transfers to gala production, from abstract management to post-congress tours.
